Apart of RMD's mission, all tools are made available to the public to validate our own work and accelerate scientific innovation through collaboration.

AvAI

AvAI is a long-term project to develope an AI that transforms information within pictures of snowpack from the Rocky Mountain snowpack dataset into useful avalanche risk predictions. The snowGAN and coreDiff are both my initial steps of training an AI what snowpack characteristics are. AvAI embeds information from both of these models alongside weather timeseries to predict avalanche risk.

The Abominable snowGAN

The snowGAN is a pre-trained GAN that has been trained on the magnefied profiles of snowpack images collected from the snowpack dataset collected through the mini coring methods. These models can be transfered to new more nuanced problems like wind loading or slab stability.

coreDiff

The coreDiff is a pre-trained model that understand features of the core samples taken apart of the snowpack dataset collected through the mini coring methods. These models can be transfered to new more nuanced problems like slab stability and segmentation.
