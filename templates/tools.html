Open-source Tools



Apart of RMD's mission, all tools are made available to the public to validate our own work and accelerate scientific innovation through collaboration.

HRfunc

HRfunc is a python library for modeling hemodynamic response function (HRF) across channels in fNIRS data, leveraging a hybrid tree and hash table data structure to enable quick look up of spatially or contextually relavent HRFs. It uses a deconvolution approach to learn an HRF distribution across a subject pool, and can be used to improve the accuracy of traditional and machine learning analysis of fNIRS.

AvAI

AvAI is a long-term project to develope an AI that transforms information within pictures of snowpack from the Rocky Mountain snowpack dataset into useful avalanche risk predictions. The snowGAN and coreDiff are both my initial steps of training an AI what snowpack characteristics are. AvAI embeds information from both of these models alongside weather timeseries to predict avalanche risk.

Pre-Trained Models

The abominable snowGAN and coreDiff are pre-trained models that understand features of the snowpack dataset collected through the mini coring methods. These models can be transfered to new more nuanced problems like wind loading or stable slab formation.
