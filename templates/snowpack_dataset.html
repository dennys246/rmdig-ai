Snowpack Data

Methodology of the snowGAN and how snowpack data was collected. Dataset and models to be released after publication of methodology to allow for proper validation by the community.



~1500 snowpack samples from two visual modalities were collected using a mini-coring method

Each sample is caputered in a chronological order and can be related in time and space to weather, geographical, and site data.

Our hope is to make the uniquely collected snowpack samples a means to explore next generation computer vision concepts to assist in avalanche risk predicion.

snowGAN and coreDiffusor are our first steps in exploring these modalities. We hope to publish a paper on the methodology of data collection and modeling, once published we will make the data freely available to download.
