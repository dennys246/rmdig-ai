Apart of RMD's mission, all data is made available to the public after publication to validate our own work and accelerate scientific innovation through collaboration.

Snowpack Dataset

The snowpack dataset was collected by Denny Schaedig through an experimental mini-coring method developed for the purpose of applying computer vision to avalanche risk assessment. The dataset contains 3,000 samples collected during the 2024 - 2025 Winter season in the Rocky Mountains of Colorado. The dataset will be made available for download in the future, once Denny can present a peer-reviewed and published paper detailing the method for collection to enable proper validation of the method itself.

Estimated to release in the Summer of 2025, mark your calendar!
