The Abominable SnowGAN

The snowGAN was an explorator model built to simply learn about snowpack data. Initially the AI was trained to simply understand features of the data, with the intention of transfering what it learned to a new task that could assist in assessing, if not predicting itself, avalanche risk. Explore applying AI to snowpack data and learning about avalanches with us by training your own AI with the dataset, or leveraging the snowGAN as a pre-trained model!
