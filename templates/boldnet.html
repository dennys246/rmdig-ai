Applying BOLDnet to Your Own Work

Apply AI to your own neuroimaging dataset by leveraging BOLDnet. This tool is designed to build custom AI architectures based on a building block structure using modern advances in AI like spatial attention. The AI is completely customizable to the task at hand, allowing you to dramatically alter the structure of your custom BOLDnet based on the depth, size and features requested in the models configuration.

To get started, follow the steps described in BOLDnet's github repository.
