Rocky Moutain Digerati's
Mission

The mission of Rocky Mountain Digerati is to provide free open-source tools and resources that empower researchers and enthusiasts in the field of neuroscience and machine learning. We aim to bridge the gap between science and artificial intelligence, enabling innovative applications and advancements in neuroimaging analysis while maintaining scientific standards. By fostering collaboration, we hope to explore the frontier of our understanding of what it means to be human.
