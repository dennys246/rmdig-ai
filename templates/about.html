About Denny Schaedig

Denny Schaedig is the founder of RMD — a Neuroscientist and AI Engineer building tools at the intersection of brain science and machine learning. He holds a Bachelor's in Neuroscience from the University of Colorado Boulder and a Master’s in AI/ML from CSU Global.

His collaborations with WashU and CU Boulder focus on the computational modeling of fMRI and fNIRS data, which led to the development of HRFunc and BOLDnet — open-source Python tools for neuroimaging preprocessing and analysis.
